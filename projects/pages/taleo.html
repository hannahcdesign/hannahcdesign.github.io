Design process and user stories

This is the ongoing design process for Oracle Taleo Performance Management Product. As an user experience designer in the team, I am responsible for creating, evaluating and modifying wireframes, high fidelity mockups and prototypes to support software application development of Taleo PM. I develop and apply software design/usability processes in the investigation of technical problems.

User research is always where we get started for our design process. One major research topic for us is trying to understand managers' pain points for doing daily work.
