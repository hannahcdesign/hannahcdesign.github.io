Research background

In this project, we developed a tool of social networks visualization for the academic literature systems - DBLP. Our design of paper-author relationship visualization facilitates both the literature and authors searching for not only professional researchers but also general users. The tool provide users with a dynamic interface for exploring academic collaboration over time and correlations crossing disciplines.
