Game concept

OIL Inc. is an oil industry simulator where a player can engage in all aspects of drilling, refining, transporting and selling oil at real world market prices. Each player starts with a bank loan of $140 million, at 6.5% interest compounded daily with 7 days of free no payment, no interest gameplay. After 7 days he must repay the debt day by day until it has all been paid, otherwise he incurs penalties.

After the investment comes in he can choose one of the career paths or engage in all 3 at the same time. The goal of the game is to make a successful oil business balancing growth, debt management, profits and expenses without going bankrupt. Insurance is provided to minimalize some of the risks that could come from random work injuries, oil spills or theft/damages to property. Insurance is however optional.

Design

Production

Deals with surveying of land, drilling and refining of crude oil extracted from the ground. Each land segment contains a limited amount of oil and once surveyed the land value automatically grows. The producer can opt for both land and offshore rigs each having individual risks and benefits. All structures are upgradable up to 10 levels.

Logistics

Deals with the transportation and storage of resources gathered from producers. A logistic player can build warehouses and shipyards connecting the producers to the brokers. He charges per ton and has to build a connecting road or pipeline to link the two players. A shipyard simply uses the water to gather offshore rig supplies.

Broker

The Broker builds franchises (gas stations) that randomly give a real world spot market price for oil. The role of the broker is to hold a portfolio of high priced gas locations around the United States and manage the costs consistently, engaging in market orders for oil and arranging for transport in order to sell and make a profit.

Syndicate

A player is free to start a syndicate, inviting other members in it and competing together for total map and market domination. Allows players to collaborate efficiently, receive bigger loans and grow faster as a group. Certain requirements are needed to invite more players while taxes also increase.

UI and data

We wanted to base the game on the resource gathering and flexibility of Star Craft, the UI and organization of Sim City and the market openness of Eve Online. All resources are gathered time based so the user is always encouraged to return frequently to gather or manage his open contracts on the market.
